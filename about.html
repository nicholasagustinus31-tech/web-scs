Our Story

Dedicated to Representing the World's Leading Media

Sinergi Cipta Selaras was founded in 2013. With a core focus on representing leading international outlets such as Bloomberg and Aviation Week, SCS has been supporting major corporations and government institutions in delivering impactful media campaigns.

Our Mission

Enable brands, public institutions, and innovators in Indonesia to gain visibility across influential media ecosystems through strategic partnerships, insightful planning, and bespoke campaign execution.

Our Approach

We combine deep market understanding with the credibility of international media partners. Every collaboration is grounded in research, cultural context, and a commitment to measurable outcomes.

• Event amplification & executive positioning
• Audience intelligence & reporting
